About Us

Welcome to Arihant — a dairy brand rooted in tradition, purity, and innovation. At Arihant, we believe that good health begins with good nutrition, and there is no better way to nurture that than through clean, wholesome dairy. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to bring farm-fresh, high-quality dairy products to homes across India while empowering rural communities and farmers. Based in India’s heartland, Arihant works closely with local dairy farmers who follow ethical and sustainable practices. Every drop of milk used in our products comes from well-cared-for cows and buffaloes, ensuring not just quality but compassion at every step. From this pure source, we craft a wide variety of products including ghee, butter, milk powders, dairy whiteners, fresh milk, curd, paneer, and beloved Indian sweets like rasgulla and gulab jamun. What sets Arihant apart is our dedication to combining traditional techniques with modern quality standards. Our ghee is slow-cooked using fresh cream to retain its natural flavor and nutrients. Our milk powders are processed with precision to deliver superior solubility and taste. Even our sweets are made in small batches to preserve authenticity and ensure the freshness that customers love. We don’t just serve individuals—we serve families, food service businesses, restaurants, retailers, and wholesalers across India. Our products cater to both B2C and B2B customers, with a commitment to timely delivery, hygienic packaging, and consistent quality. At Arihant, quality control is more than just a process—it's a promise. Every batch undergoes rigorous testing and meets FSSAI standards, and we maintain cold-chain logistics to keep freshness intact from farm to shelf. Whether it’s a spoon of ghee for your daily meal or a paneer block for a restaurant kitchen, Arihant ensures it is 100% pure, safe, and nourishing. Sustainability is also close to our heart. We use eco-conscious packaging wherever possible and continually explore ways to reduce our carbon footprint. Our operations provide fair wages, training, and consistent income to our network of farmers, making Arihant a brand that uplifts lives and communities. In a world filled with artificial flavours and shortcuts, Arihant stands tall as a brand you can trust. Every product you receive is made with honesty, love, and respect for nature. As we grow, we remain focused on our vision—to be India’s most loved and trusted dairy brand, one that honours the journey from farm to family. Thank you for choosing Arihant. Together, let’s build a healthier tomorrow, one spoon at a time.

Our Mission

  • To deliver pure and nutritious dairy products from farm to table.
  • To uplift local farmers by ensuring fair prices and steady demand.
  • To lead innovation in dairy processing while maintaining tradition.
  • To promote sustainable and ethical dairy practices.
  • To educate consumers on health benefits of pure dairy.
  • To build a reliable supply chain ensuring product freshness.
  • To empower rural communities through employment.
  • To ensure transparency and traceability in every batch.
  • To become a globally trusted Indian dairy brand.

Our Vision

  • To be a household name known for purity and quality in dairy.
  • To revolutionize dairy access through tech-driven systems.
  • To minimize adulteration and ensure food safety.
  • To create India's most transparent dairy sourcing model.
  • To expand into international markets with Indian dairy values.
  • To invest in R&D for innovative dairy-based products.
  • To set benchmarks for ethical dairy manufacturing.
  • To build strong, long-lasting consumer relationships.
  • To shape a healthier, protein-rich India through dairy.
